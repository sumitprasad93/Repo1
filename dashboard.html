Connect Your Bank via Tink

Tink is a licensed Open Banking platform (PSD2 / EU). Read-only access — your credentials are never shared with us.

1
Authorization — You'll be redirected to Tink's secure portal to pick your bank.
2
Bank Login — Authenticate directly with your bank (SCA / 2FA).
3
Token Exchange — Tink issues an authorization_code → our backend swaps it for an access_token.
4
Data Sync — We fetch your accounts & transactions via GET /data/v2/accounts and /transactions.
